# TASK 4(B): Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
## CREATE A SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS CHECKLIST:

DO’S:
•	Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate. 
•	Maintain privacy settings to protect your personal information. 
•	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation. 
•	Use professional language, especially on academic or career-related platforms. 
•	Be mindful of your digital footprint—it can impact future opportunities. 

DON’TS:
•	Don’t share sensitive personal details like address or phone number publicly. 
•	Don’t engage in online arguments or post offensive comments. 
•	Don’t spread rumors, fake news, or unverified content. 
•	Don’t post inappropriate photos or content that could harm your reputation. 
•	Don’t ignore cybersecurity—avoid clicking suspicious links or messages.
