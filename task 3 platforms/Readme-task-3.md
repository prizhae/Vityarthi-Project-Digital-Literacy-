Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz

A simple educational activity created using Google Forms to promote awareness of digital literacy, online safety, and responsible internet usage.


 Project Overview

The Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz is designed as an interactive quiz form containing 5 multiple-choice questions.  
It helps users understand essential concepts related to digital literacy and encourages safe and ethical behavior in the digital world.

This activity can be used for:
- Academic submission
- Awareness campaigns
- School/college digital literacy programs
- Beginner-level online safety education



  Objectives

The main objectives of this activity are to:

- Create awareness about digital literacy
- Promote safe and responsible internet use
- Help users identify online risks and threats
- Encourage critical thinking before sharing information online
- Improve understanding of digital citizenship



  Quiz Details

- Platform Used: Google Forms
- Quiz Title: Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz
- Number of Questions: 5
- Question Type: Multiple Choice
- Difficulty Level: Beginner / Awareness Level

  Quiz Questions Included

The quiz contains the following five questions:

1. What is the definition of digital literacy ?**
2. Which of the following is the reputable source for research?
3. What is considered as a "Digital Footprint"?
4. What is the main goal of "Data Privacy"?
5. Why is digital literacy considered an essential life skill in the 21st century?
