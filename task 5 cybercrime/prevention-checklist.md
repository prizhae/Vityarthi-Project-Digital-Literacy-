# Task 5: Cybercrime Case Study and Prevention Checklist

## Part B: Prevention Checklist – Stay Safe Online

- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Do not share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Always verify links before clicking, especially those received via SMS, email, or messaging apps.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts like banking or email.
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
- Be cautious while downloading apps—only install from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store.
- UPI Safety: Double-check the recipient’s name before sending money through UPI apps.
- UPI Safety: Never approve “collect requests” unless you are sure of the sender’s identity.
- Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public computers.
- Immediately report suspicious transactions or cyber fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Helpline 1930.
